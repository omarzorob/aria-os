## What Is Aria OS?

Aria OS is an AI-native Android agent that turns your phone into a true autonomous assistant. Unlike Siri or Google Assistant ‚Äî which are sophisticated search engines ‚Äî Aria is a full reasoning agent. It receives natural language commands, plans multi-step execution paths, and uses a comprehensive tool registry to actually get things done on your device.

**Example:**
> "Order me my usual coffee from Starbucks"

Aria doesn't open a browser. It opens the Starbucks app, navigates to your saved order, selects the nearest pickup location, applies your rewards points, and confirms payment ‚Äî all in under 10 seconds.
